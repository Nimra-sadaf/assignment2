spaghetti recipe

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g tomato sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Saut√© the onion and garlic until soft.
  3. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in the tomato sauce, and let it simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve the sauce over the spaghetti and garnish with Parmesan cheese.
